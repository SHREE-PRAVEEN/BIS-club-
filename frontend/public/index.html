Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to common questions about BIS 2025.

🔍

🎯 Orientation Week

What happens during BIS 2025 orientation week? +

Orientation includes campus tours, academic advising sessions, social activities and Yoga, information sessions about resources and services, club and organization fairs, welcome ceremony, technology setup assistance, and sessions on college life skills and safety.

Is attendance mandatory for all orientation activities? +

While most activities are highly recommended, some sessions like academic advising and safety briefings are mandatory. Social activities are optional but strongly encouraged as they help you meet fellow students and build connections that will enhance your college experience.

🏠 Campus Life & Facilities

What facilities are available on campus? +

The campus offers a wide range of facilities to support student life and learning, including well-equipped libraries, computer labs with high-speed internet, sports complexes, student hostels, and dining halls. Common rooms within the hostels offer spaces for relaxation and socializing, equipped with indoor activities like table tennis and more to keep students engaged. Additionally, there are counseling services and event venues to enhance the overall campus experience.

How do I get around campus? +

The campus is designed to be student-friendly and walkable, with clear signage and pathways connecting all major buildings. Most academic blocks, hostels, dining areas, and recreational spaces are within easy walking distance, making it convenient to get around.

What are the campus safety measures? +

The campus is equipped with 24/7 security through trained personnel, CCTV surveillance, and controlled entry/exit points. Hostel wardens and the Student Affairs team are available for support. Emergency protocols are in place, including medical aid and evacuation plans. The campus prioritizes student safety with a zero-tolerance policy for harassment or misconduct.

What kind of fun and recreational activities are available on campus? +

The campus offers a wide range of fun and recreational activities, including cultural nights, open mics, sports tournaments, movie screenings, festivals, and club events. Fun sessions are organized frequently to help students unwind, connect, and showcase their talents. From indoor games like table tennis and carrom in common rooms to interest-based clubs and casual hangouts, there's always something exciting happening to keep the campus lively and engaging!

🏠 Accommodation & Housing

What types of accommodation are available? +

We offer housing options Triple shared rooms, apartment-style housing for first Years. All rooms come with basic furniture, internet access, and utilities included. Some buildings offer air conditioning and premium amenities.

What are the hostel rules and regulations? +

Newly admitted students must be inside the hostel premises by 06:30 PM; hostel gates will be closed at 01:00 AM sharp. Team BIS members must return to their rooms by 8:00 AM, unless they have explicit permission to stay out later from the Head - Student Affairs. Attendance must be given during the dinner break. ⚠️ Important: Smoking, consumption of alcohol, and use of any narcotic substances are strictly prohibited anywhere on the campus of REC, including hostels and outdoor areas. Electrical appliances such as irons, straighteners, etc., are not allowed inside hostel rooms. However, students can use the common facilities provided in the common room, which include a fridge, microwave, stove, and iron for everyone's convenience.

How do laundry facilities work? +

There is one laundromat on campus that operates through a dedicated mobile app. You can book machines, check availability, and get notified when your laundry is done. Please note that each wash requires payment through the app.

Hostel timings for Aarambh 2025 +
  • Newly admitted students must be inside hostels by 11:30 PM; hostel gates will be closed at 12:00 AM.
  • Team Aarambh members must be back in their rooms by 1:00 AM, unless required to stay out later by the Head - Student Affairs.
  • Attendance to be given during dinner break

🍽️ Dining & Meal Plans

What dining options are available on campus? +

We have multiple dining halls offering four meals daily: breakfast, lunch, evening snacks, and dinner. Vegetarian meals are included, while non-vegetarian options are available separately on a paid basis and kept separate from vegetarian food. Vending machines are placed throughout campus, and the cafeteria is open with delivery services available for hostels.

How do meal plans work? +

Students can choose from various meal plans ranging from 10 meals per week to unlimited access. Meals are accessed using your student ID card, and unused meals can sometimes roll over. Flex dollars can be used at campus eateries and convenience stores.
